Last updated: March 15, 2026

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you use Cosmic Help, you may provide us with the following information during the onboarding process: your name or nickname, date of birth, city of birth, gender, relationship status, life focus areas, and astrology experience level. This information is used solely to personalize your cosmic guidance and personalized recommendations.

Camera and Photo Data

Cosmic Help uses your device camera to capture images of coffee cups, palms, and faces for AI-powered readings. These images are sent to our AI processing service (powered by Anthropic's Claude API via Supabase Edge Functions) for analysis. Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers. Images are transmitted securely via HTTPS encryption.

We may collect anonymous usage data such as app interaction patterns, feature usage frequency, and crash reports to improve our services. This data does not personally identify you.

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). We use RevenueCat as our subscription management platform. We do not directly collect or store your payment information such as credit card numbers. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase-related data as described in their privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: provide personalized fortune readings, horoscopes, and interpretations; improve and optimize our AI reading algorithms; process your subscription and manage your account; communicate with you about app updates or changes; and comply with legal obligations.

Data Storage and Security

Your profile information is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. Reading history is stored locally on your device. Images sent for AI analysis are processed in real-time through Supabase Edge Functions and Anthropic's Claude API. We do not maintain a central database of your personal readings or images. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

Cosmic Help uses the following third-party services:

Anthropic (Claude API): For AI-powered fortune reading interpretations. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data processed through their API. Visit: anthropic.com/privacy

Supabase: For serverless API functions. Visit: supabase.com/privacy

RevenueCat: For subscription management. Visit: revenuecat.com/privacy

Cosmic Help is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal information we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate information; request deletion of your data (you can reset your profile within the app at any time); opt out of data collection by uninstalling the application.

Data Retention

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. Deleting the app or resetting your profile within the app will remove all locally stored data. AI-processed images are not retained after the reading is complete.
