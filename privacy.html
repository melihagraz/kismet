Cosmic Help - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Cosmic Help collects the following information to provide personalized cosmic guidance:

  • Profile Information: Name, birth date, city, gender, relationship status, life focus areas
  • Authentication: Apple ID or Google account (via Sign In with Apple / Google)
  • Photos: Only when you voluntarily upload a profile photo or submit images for AI analysis (coffee cup, palm, face)
  • Usage Data: App interactions to improve the experience

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized cosmic guidance and weekly recommendations
  • Match compatible users (only for users who opt in to Cosmic Matching)
  • Generate AI-powered birth chart interpretations
  • Improve app features and user experience

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). Profile data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Photos are stored in secure cloud storage.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): To generate personalized readings and guidance. No personally identifiable information is sent — only anonymized profile context.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • Apple: For authentication (Sign In with Apple)

5. Cosmic Matching

If you opt in to Cosmic Matching, your name, zodiac sign, city, age, and optional bio/photo will be visible to your matched users. You can opt out at any time.

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at cosmichelp.app@gmail.com. Data will be deleted within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Cosmic Help is not intended for users under 17 years of age.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
